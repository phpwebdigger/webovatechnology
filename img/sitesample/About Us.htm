Web Development

The process and practice of developing web applications is known as the Web application development. However, it is a risky process at times. The web applications have varying levels of risk. There are several technologies involved in the Web application development process such as Ajax, CSS, JSP, JavaScript, PHP, HTML5 . There are several factors that influence the web-based business such as Extreme programming, Scrum, Timebox development and Feature-driven development. Enterprise Portal Development

Enterprise Portal Development is a process that involves development of an enterprise portal, also known as an enterprise information portal. It provides a secure unified access point, sometimes in the form of a web-based user interface, and also designed to aggregate and personalize information through application-specific portlets. The de-centralized content contribution and content management, which keeps the information always updated as well as the catering for customers, vendors and others beyond an organization's boundaries are two special characters of the Enterprise portal.Electronic business, or e-business, is the application of information and communication technologies (ICT) in support of all the activities of business and the Electronic business solutions is the solution to all the problems related to the online business. Online Advertising

Online Advertising is also known as Internet advertising. It is used to deliver promotional marketing messages to the consumers. It includes email marketing, search engine marketing, social media marketing, many types of display advertising (including web banner advertising), and mobile advertising. It frequently involves both a publisher, who integrates advertisements into its online content, and an advertiser, who provides the advertisements to be displayed on the publisher’s content. Other potential participants include advertising agencies that help generate and place the ad copy, an ad server who technologically delivers the ad and tracks statistics, and advertising affiliates who do independent promotional work for the advertiser.
